We are writing to inform you of a lost item recently found on school grounds. A sum of 30,000 Naira was discovered in the playground.

We understand that losing such a significant amount of money can be very stressful.

We are committed to reuniting this lost item with its rightful owner.

Here's How to Claim the Lost Money:

Description

The money is in the form of various denominations.

Claim Process

To claim the lost money, please come to the school office. When you arrive, please be prepared to:

  • Describe the exact amount and denominations of the lost money.
  • Provide your child's name and class.
  • Answer any additional questions that may help us verify your claim.

Confidentiality

We understand the sensitive nature of this situation. All inquiries and claims will be handled with the utmost discretion.

Please Share This Announcement

If you know a nursery or primary school student at our school who may have lost this money, please share this announcement with them or their parents.

Time Limit for Claim

The unclaimed money will be held by the school for 7 days. If no valid claim is made within this timeframe, the school will donate the funds to a local charity that supports the education of underprivileged children.

Conclusion

We hope to reunite this lost money with its owner as soon as possible. Please don't hesitate to contact the school office at 09123456789 if you have any questions.
