School Picture day is coming up on 12th June 2023.

Mark your calendars, parents! It's time to capture those adorable school smiles again. We're thrilled to announce that Picture Day for our amazing primary school students is coming up on Monday, June 12th, 2023.

Capture the Memories:

These pictures are precious keepsakes that will be cherished for years to come. Imagine them tucked away in a family album, proudly displayed on a mantle, or even immortalized in the school yearbook.

Preparing Your Child:

  • Picture Perfect Attire: We encourage students to dress in their school uniforms for a classic and unified look. However, feel free to add a touch of personality with a favorite school-appropriate accessory.
  • Hair and Smiles: A neat and tidy hairstyle will help your child feel confident in their picture. Don't forget to remind them to practice their brightest smiles!
  • Ordering Information: Detailed information on how to order individual and class pictures will be sent home shortly. It will include deadlines, package options, and online ordering instructions.

Making Picture Day Smooth:

  • Schedule: We will be sending out a detailed schedule closer to Picture Day that outlines specific times for each class.
  • Pre-payment Option (Optional): For your convenience, some schools offer a pre-payment option for picture packages. Please refer to the information packet for details.
  • Absences: If your child is absent on Picture Day, don't worry! We will have a make-up day scheduled. Information on the make-up day will be communicated to parents separately.

Conclusion

We look forward to capturing these special moments for you and your child!

Should you have any questions regarding Picture Day, please don't hesitate to contact your child's teacher or the school office.
