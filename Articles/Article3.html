Exams are around the corner.

This is a friendly reminder that Exams are just around the corner! We understand this can be an exciting yet stressful time for both students and parents. To ensure a smooth exam experience

please take note of the following important information and reminders:

School Fees

A gentle reminder that all outstanding school fees must be cleared before your child can participate in the exams. Please contact the school office at 09123456789 if you have any questions regarding fees.

Stationery

Please ensure your child brings a labeled water bottle and a healthy snack for exam breaks. Electronic devices are strictly prohibited in the exam hall.

Early Arrival

We encourage students to arrive at school at least 7:40am before their exams to avoid any last-minute rush.

Positive Reinforcement

Exams can be stressful, so please offer your child encouragement and support throughout their preparation. Celebrate their hard work and focus on their learning journey, not just the final results.

Conclusion

We understand the importance of a strong partnership between school and home. By working together, we can ensure your child approaches these exams with confidence and feels prepared to showcase their knowledge and skills.
