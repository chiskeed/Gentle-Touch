Non-uniform day on 25th June 2024.

Calling all parents of nursery and primary school students! Get ready to break out the comfy clothes and unleash some creativity – we're having a Non-Uniform Day on Friday, June 25th, 2024!

Here's what you need to know:

A Day for Fun and Fundraising

This non-uniform day is more than just a chance for your child to ditch their school uniform. It's also a fun way to raise funds for a new playground!

Dress Up or Dress Down

The choice is yours! Let your child express their individuality by dressing up in a costume (as long as it's school-appropriate, of course!), or keep it comfy in their favorite pair of jeans and a t-shirt. We encourage a dress code that is active-friendly as there may be fun activities planned throughout the day.

Important Reminders

  • Donations Welcome: A small donation is encouraged to support our fundraising efforts. You can send the donation in a labeled envelope with your child.
  • Weather-Appropriate Clothing: Please keep the weather forecast in mind when choosing your child's outfit.
  • Label Everything: With all the excitement, misplaced belongings can happen. Don't forget to label your child's clothes, water bottle, and any other belongings they bring to school.

Personal Growth and Empathy

Reading exposes you to diverse experiences, cultures, and perspectives, fostering personal growth and empathy. Through literature, you can walk in the shoes of characters from different backgrounds and gain insight into their lives and struggles. This broadens your worldview, cultivates empathy and understanding, and nurtures qualities essential for global citizenship.

Conclusion

We're looking forward to seeing all the creative and fun outfits on Non-Uniform Day! It's a fantastic opportunity to raise money for a great cause while celebrating our amazing school community.
