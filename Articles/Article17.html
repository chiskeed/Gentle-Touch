Spelling Bee Competition Will Take Place at Abuja

Attention parents of primary school students in Abuja! Is your child a whiz with words? Do they relish the challenge of spelling bee competitions? If so, then get ready to cheer them on because the Abuja Spelling Bee Competition is back, and it's searching for its next champion!

Calling Young Wordsmiths!

This prestigious competition is a fantastic platform for young minds to showcase their exceptional spelling skills and vocabulary knowledge. It's open to students in Grades 3-5.

Why Participate?

The Abuja Spelling Bee Competition offers a multitude of benefits for your child:

  • Boosts Confidence: Successfully navigating challenging words and emerging victorious can significantly enhance your child's self-belief.
  • Sharpens Vocabulary: Preparing for the competition will broaden your child's vocabulary and expose them to new and interesting words.
  • Improves Communication Skills: Strong spelling skills are the foundation for effective communication, both written and verbal.
  • It's Fun!: Learning doesn't have to be dull! The competition provides a stimulating and engaging environment for children to learn and compete.

Competition Details

  • Location: The competition will be held in Abuja at NO.12, Mr. Mark's Avenue, Gwagwalada.
  • Date: Mark your calendars for 7th July!
  • Registration: The deadline to register your child is 4th July. For registration details and fees, please call 09123456789.

Conclusion

The Abuja Spelling Bee Competition promises to be a thrilling event filled with excitement and intellectual challenge. We encourage all parents of primary school students in Abuja to register their children and give them the opportunity to shine!
