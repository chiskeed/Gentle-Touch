Open House for New Parents

Are you a new parent? We're thrilled to welcome you and your child to our vibrant school community! To help you get acquainted with everything our school has to offer, we're hosting a special Open House just for you. On 30th July 2024

What to Expect:

Meet the Principal and Staff

This is your chance to mingle with our school principal, teachers, and support staff. They're eager to introduce themselves, answer your questions, and share their passion for education.

Explore the School

Take a guided tour of our facilities, including classrooms, playgrounds, libraries, and specialist areas like art and music studios.

Curriculum and Programs

Learn about our engaging curriculum, exciting extracurricular activities, and the support programs we offer to ensure every child thrives.

Conclusion

In our school, we believe in fostering strong partnerships between parents and educators. We have an open-door policy, so please feel free to reach out to your child's teacher, the principal, or any staff member with questions or concerns throughout the year.
